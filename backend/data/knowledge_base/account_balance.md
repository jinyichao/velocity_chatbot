# Account Balance

## How to check account balance
Log in to OCBC Velocity > Accounts > Account Summary. All linked business accounts are listed with their current available balance and ledger balance.

## Available balance vs ledger balance
- **Available balance**: Funds immediately available for use, after holds and pending transactions.
- **Ledger balance**: Total balance including uncleared transactions.

## Multi-currency accounts
Velocity displays each currency sub-account balance separately under the parent account.

## Balance as of cut-off time
Balances reflect end-of-day positions after OCBC's daily cut-off at 11:59 PM SGT.

## Insufficient funds alert
You can set up low-balance alerts under Alerts & Notifications so Velocity notifies you when the balance falls below a threshold.
