# Cheque Services

## Order a cheque book
Go to Cheque Services > Order Cheque Book. Select the account and quantity (1 book = 50 leaves). Delivery to registered business address takes 5–7 business days.

## Stop a cheque
Go to Cheque Services > Stop Cheque. Enter the cheque number(s) and reason. Stop instruction takes effect immediately for uncleared cheques.

## Stop payment fee
A stop cheque fee applies per cheque stopped. Refer to OCBC's Business Banking Fee Schedule for current rates.

## Cheque status enquiry
Check whether a specific cheque has been cleared under Cheque Services > Cheque Enquiry. Enter the cheque number to see status (cleared/outstanding/stopped).

## Cheque return
Cheques returned unpaid (e.g. insufficient funds) are notified via alert (if set up) and reflected in Transaction History with the return reason.

## Post-dated cheques
Velocity cannot hold post-dated cheques electronically. Physical post-dated cheques presented for clearing will be processed on the cheque date.

## Cheque clearing time
Cheques deposited before 3:30 PM SGT on business days are typically cleared the next business day.
