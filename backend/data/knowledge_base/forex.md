# Foreign Exchange (FX)

## Supported currencies
Velocity supports FX transactions in major currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, AUD, CNY, HKD, MYR, THB, and others.

## FX conversion
Go to Foreign Exchange > FX Conversion. Select source account, target currency, amount, and value date. Indicative rates are displayed before confirmation.

## Rate validity
Indicative FX rates are valid for approximately 30 seconds. Lock in the rate by confirming within the timer.

## Value dates
- **Spot**: T+2 settlement (standard)
- **Tom**: T+1 settlement
- **Today (TOD)**: Same-day, subject to cut-off times

## FX cut-off times (SGT)
- USD, EUR, GBP: 4:00 PM
- JPY, AUD, HKD: 3:00 PM
- MYR: 12:00 PM

## FX limit order
Available for select currency pairs. Set a target rate and the system will execute automatically when the market rate is reached.

## Hedging
Forward FX contracts (FX Forward) are available for rate-locking future transactions. Contact your Relationship Manager or submit a request via Velocity.
