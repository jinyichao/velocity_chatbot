# Trade Finance

## Products available on Velocity
- Letter of Credit (LC) – Import and Export
- Trust Receipt (TR) financing
- Shipping Guarantee
- Documentary Collection
- Bank Guarantee
- Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC)

## Applying for a Letter of Credit
Go to Trade Finance > Letter of Credit > Apply for Import LC. Fill in the beneficiary details, shipment terms, and required documents. Submit for bank review.

## Trust Receipt (TR)
TR financing allows drawdown against import documents. Apply under Trade Finance > Trust Receipt. Approval is subject to existing TR facility limits.

## Checking trade facility limits
Available limits are displayed under Trade Finance > Facility Summary.

## Document submission
Physical trade documents can be submitted at any OCBC Business Banking Centre. For digitised documents, use Velocity's document upload function where available.

## Status tracking
Track the status of all submitted trade transactions under Trade Finance > Transaction Status.

## Fees and charges
Trade finance fees (LC issuance, amendment, negotiation) are detailed in OCBC's Business Banking Fee Schedule available on the OCBC website.
