# Bill Payment

## Supported bill types
Velocity supports payment to government agencies (IRAS, CPF, HDB, URA), utilities (SP Group, PUB), telcos, and other GIRO-registered billers.

## Adding a biller
Go to Bill Payment > Manage Billers > Add Biller. Biller details (e.g. GST reference, account number) are needed. New billers require Maker-Checker approval.

## One-time vs recurring payments
- **One-time**: Submit a payment immediately or schedule for a future date.
- **GIRO recurring**: Set up under Bill Payment > GIRO Arrangements. Requires the biller's GIRO form to be submitted to OCBC.

## GIRO cut-off
GIRO bill payments submitted before 10:30 PM SGT on business days are processed the next business day.

## Payment confirmation
A payment reference number is issued upon successful submission. Retrieve it under Transaction History.

## Cancellation
Scheduled (not yet processed) payments can be cancelled under Bill Payment > Scheduled Payments.
