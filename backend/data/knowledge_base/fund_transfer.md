# Fund Transfer

## Transfer types supported
- **FAST**: Real-time, 24/7, SGD only, up to SGD 200,000 per transaction.
- **GIRO**: Next-business-day, SGD, suitable for recurring payments.
- **TT (Telegraphic Transfer)**: International wire transfer, multi-currency.
- **MEPS+**: High-value SGD transfers (above SGD 200,000), processed same day.
- **PayNow Corporate**: Transfer using UEN or PayNow-linked account.

## Daily transfer limits
Default limits can be viewed and adjusted (subject to approval) under Settings > Transfer Limits. Changes require Maker-Checker authorisation.

## Maker-Checker workflow
Transfers above the auto-approval threshold require a second authoriser (Checker) to approve before submission. Pending transactions appear in the Pending Authorisation queue.

## Recurring transfers
Set up standing instructions under Fund Transfer > Standing Instructions. Frequency options: daily, weekly, fortnightly, monthly.

## Cut-off times
- FAST: Immediate, 24/7
- GIRO: 10:30 PM SGT for next-business-day credit
- TT: 4:00 PM SGT for same-day processing (business days)
- MEPS+: 3:30 PM SGT for same-day settlement
