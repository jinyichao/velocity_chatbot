# Account Statement

## Download e-statement
Go to Accounts > Statements. Select the account, year, and month, then download as PDF.

## Statement frequency
Monthly statements are generated on the last business day of each month and available within 2 business days.

## Availability
E-statements are available for up to 5 years online. Older statements can be requested via OCBC Business Customer Service (fees may apply).

## Certified statements
For certified/bank-stamped statements, submit a request through Velocity > Service Requests > Statement Request. Turnaround is 3–5 business days.

## SWIFT statement (MT940)
Available for current accounts. Download under Statements > SWIFT Statements for ERP reconciliation.

## Auto-email delivery
Configure automatic monthly statement delivery to designated email addresses under Settings > Statement Preferences.
